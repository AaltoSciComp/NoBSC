Registration

Option 1: There is no registration fee, but you have to pay for your own food, snacks, and coffee.

Option 2: We have a registration fee designed to cover just the cost of your food, snacks, dinner, and coffee. (ASC covers fixed costs of the facilities.)

We start with option 1 and option 2 is used during reconfirmation if we get enough registrations.
